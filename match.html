EXACT Match - Lab

Real-time Curing Chamber for concrete cylinders


Match is a concrete match-curing system that creates a controlled environment for curing concrete samples, replicating real-time site conditions. The device functions as a custom-designed environmental chamber, pulling real-time setpoints from the cloud via other EXACT products.

As the sole mechanical designer for most of the project, I was responsible for the complete mechanical design and physical validation testing. My solution featured a custom metal enclosure with a rear-mounted TEC unit, while an internal metal cage optimized airflow to ensure uniform temperature distribution.

Serviceability was a critical design factor, as failures could lead to project delays.
